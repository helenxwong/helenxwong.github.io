How to Find a Variegated Monstera

This incredible plant is called a Variegated Monstera. What makes this plant so special is that it shows two different colors on the same leaf. This is caused by a special genetic mutation, it shows two different chromosomal make-ups in a single plant, where some tissue is able to produce chlorophyll and other is not

Every plant lover will know how hard it is to find the Variegated Monstera. Local stores will not carry such a rare breed, ordering online or through verified plant stores on Instagram are both great ways. If you are able to travel, book a plan ticket to Portland Oregon.
