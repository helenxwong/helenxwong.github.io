Our Story

Since the invention of bubble tea in the 1980s, it spread all over the world and became the most popular Taiwanese drink. Before Bubbly Boba was established, every bubble tea shop would only serve one type of tapioca pearl, the honey black boba, a prepackaged tapioca that can store for 8 months or longer. We wanted to break this tradition and show our creativity and passion in boba, and that was when Bubbly Boba was found.
