Our Story

The Bubbly Boba Experience

Bubbly Vibes and Drinks Made Right

Since the invention of bubble tea in the 1980s, it spread all over the world and became the most popular Taiwanese drink. Before Bubbly Boba was established, every bubble tea shop would only serve one type of tapioca pearl, the honey black boba, a prepackaged tapioca that can store for 8 months or longer. We wanted to break this tradition and show our creativity and passion in boba, and that was when Bubbly Boba was found.

We came up with the idea of making boba in our own store so everyone could experience how fresh boba tastes. Although it took countless trials and failure to create different boba flavors, we became the world's first bubble tea brand to make fresh boba in-store. While our boba does not have the typical long shelf life, we hope you will feel our passion and enjoy our boba.
